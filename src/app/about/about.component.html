About OIP

Open Innovation Platform (OIP) is an initiative from Telekom R&D Sdn. Bhd. to realize the potentials of Internet-of-Things (IoT) in digital era. End-to end IoT solution, including transport messaging protocols, data storing and retrieving, security and rapid application development, is make simple through OIP. Beside, right sets of communication, information and intelligence services either from telcos' or third parties' network assets can also be securely exposed using network Application Program Interfaces (APIs) of OIP. Application developers can now simply focus their energy on their innovation ideas and service launching. OIP as TM's digital platform is here to help you to achieve your business goals.

What do we offer on OIP platform?

Rapid go-to-market platform for developers to deliver smart IoT services

The platform’s for developers to access the data and the services

The platform’s for developers to publish the data and the created services

Developer focus on application development and OIP will take care of IT infrastructure, security and scalability for your IOT Project
